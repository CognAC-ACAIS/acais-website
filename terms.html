2. Registering tickets

  1. Tickets can only be bought through “acais.nl” or https://tinyurl.com/acais2017”.
  2. A ticket is registered to the individual that fills in the order information (the ‘attendee’).
  3. Tickets cannot be exchanged without consent from the organization.
  4. A separate dinner ticket can be bought by the attendee using the same link. The same Terms and Conditions apply for this ticket.
  5. The attendee is responsible for the correct presentation of a ticket at the registration of the symposium itself (on May the 10th). If the attendee cannot produce a valid ticket at the registration of the symposium, the committee has the right to withhold access to the attendee.

3. Ticket Types

  1. There are discounted tickets available for specific groups.The organization has the right to deny entrance to the event when the attendee cannot show his or her membership to the specific group.
  2. Groups who are eligible for a discount are:
  • CognAC Member: limited to members and benefactors of CognAC.
  • NSVKI Member: limited to (non Radboud University) AI students.
  • RU Student/Staff: limited to Radboud University students and staff. A valid student or employee card is required.
  • Student: Non-Radboud University students. A valid student card is required.
  • Alumni RU AI, limited to alumni of Artificial Intelligence (former ‘Cognitiewetenschappen’) at the Radboud University.

4. Withdrawal of tickets

  1. Tickets can be cancelled three days in advance of the event by sending an email with the transaction id to the organization. After this deadline a refund of a ticket is not possible due to reservations and the registration required at the ACAIS 2017 venue.
  2. When a withdrawal is accepted by the organisation the ticket price will be refunded.
  3. Exceptions can be made for specific cases, please contact us at symposium@svcognac.nl with questions regarding ticket withdrawal.
