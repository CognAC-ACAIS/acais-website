What is ACAIS?

Each year the symposium committee of study association CognAC organises ACAIS: the Annual CognAC AI Symposium. Related to a general theme, multiple researchers, organisations and companies present their research and projects. Our goal is to organise a day which combines many of the disciplines regarding AI.
