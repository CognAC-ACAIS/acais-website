SurvAIval (2017)

Date: 10th of May, 2017

AI is becoming more and more well known and relevant, and many people worry about the future of humans as a species. This is why we present ACAIS 2017: SurvAIval, where talks will be given related to this.

Topics:

  • Beyond AR and the Internet of Things
  • AI & Law: an overview from an argumentation perspective
  • Adaptive Educational Apps for Self-taught Literacy and Numeracy
  • The TRADR project: applied Human-Robot Teaming in the context of Search & Rescue
  • Can AI save lives in traffic?
  • SuvAIval of the slickest? Superintelligence, cyborgs and human remains
