Dear Alumnus,

It may have been long ago, or relatively recent, but without a doubt you remember studying Cognitive Science or Artificial Intelligence at the Radboud University in Nijmegen. The joys of homework, those wonderful exams, these great teachers, your exquisite fellow students, your youth…

Well, soon you can relive or revive those wonderful memories, at the AI/CogSci alumni day on Saturday, the 28th of May, 2016. Yes, in the very same building, yes with the very same (well… slightly older teachers), and yes, with classes and an EXAM!!

Considering the importance of life-long-learning, we thought it wise to make this day not only fun, but also informative (say, a bit like your original study program). Hence, we organize (apart from coffee, drinks and a buffet) a workshop where you get updated on the most recent, cutting-edge, research on Deep Neural Nets. In four hours you will learn about the latest theory, engage in experimentation, and receive your Life-Long-Learning Diploma!! (that is, if you pass the exam…)

Join us!

Workshop


Deep Learning

Artificial neural networks have regained popularity in recent years. They have transformed research in computer vision, natural language processing, gaming and robotics. In our own work artificial neural networks are used to probe human brain function at an unprecedented level of accuracy. In this workshop you will learn about the basics of modern neural networks and their applications. During the subsequent practical you will implement and apply neural networks yourself using the Matlab language. Finally your newly acquired knowledge will be tested using an old-fashioned closed-book exam.


Recommended literature: LeCun, Y., Bengio, Y., Hinton, G., 2015. Deep learning. Nature 521, 436–444. doi:10.1038/nature14539
