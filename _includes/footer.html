ACAIS 2015

ACAIS 2015 is an annual symposium organised by a team of students, all studying Artificial Intelligence at the Radboud University. ACAIS and the committee are part of study association CognAC. Our philosophy is for and by students; organsing an event that stimulates new insights and the exchange of information.

ACAIS is non-profit and is fully funded by ticket fees, subsidies and sponsorships.
