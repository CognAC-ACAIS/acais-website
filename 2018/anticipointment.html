Is it good to have positive expectations about a social event, such as ACAIS 2018? Or can it save us a lot of disappointment if we expected the worst? This is but one question that can be answered with the help of text mining. In this talk I will advocate the utility of text mining, and introduce some far from solved tasks. After hearing the talk you might end up motivated to help the text mining field further.

...or just experience #anticipointment.
